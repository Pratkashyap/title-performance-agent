Q: Why is Euphoria S2 underperforming in Southeast Asia?

Performance Summary

Euphoria S2 is significantly underperforming in Southeast Asia, with a completion rate of 41.2% against the Drama genre benchmark of 63.8% — a gap of 22.6 percentage points. Total starts across SG, TH, PH, MY are 84,300 over the last 30 days, but only 34,700 completions were recorded.

Key Finding

  • Episode 3 is the critical drop-off point — 34% of viewers who started Ep 3 abandon at the ~48% mark, suggesting the narrative pacing in the middle act is misaligned with SEA viewer behaviour patterns
  • SG performs worst at 38.4% completion; MY is strongest at 47.1% but still below benchmark
  • The underperformance is isolated to SEA — AU and HK show 61.2% and 58.9% completion respectively, near benchmark

Recommended Actions

  • Investigate localisation quality for Ep 3 in SEA subtitle tracks — pacing issues often amplified by translation lag
  • Consider promotional push at Ep 3 → 4 transition for SG/TH markets to reduce drop-off
  • Review thumbnail/autoplay preview for SEA — may be setting incorrect content expectation
